Welcome


Shapovalov Scientific Publishing OÜ is an Estonian Scholarly Publisher available internationally. It was founded in 2021 in Estonia and publishes peer-reviewed indexed journals, books and thesis. Our main goal is to spread Open Science through the publication in open access of scientific articles. We focus on the following scientific fields: medicine, pharmacy, jurisprudence, engineering, social science and information technologies.

In 2022 we have gained Sponsorship Status with CrossRef and began to provide to our clients a lot of new services to give everything you need in your work.

We have multilingual team of native speakers of various languages to provide you with the best support possible. Please, feel free to contact us if you have more questions
